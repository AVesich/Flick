Manage & arrange your windows with a flick of your trackpad.

Flick lets you intuitively manage & arrange the windows on your Mac without moving a hand from the trackpad. Flick runs natively with impeccable performance, uses intuitive & familiar gestures, and runs entirely offline.

Flick supports Intel & Apple Silicon Macs, tested on MacOS 15.0+ (Sequoia)

FAQ

  1. Does Flick store any data, and does it transmit this to anyone?

    The only data Flick stores are a few numeric or true/false values for your preferences, but nothing related to you as a person or how you use the app. Do not give your personal data to anything claiming to be Flick on your Mac.
    Flick connects to the internet only when registering/downgrading your license and does not send any data back to the developer or any other person/company.

  2. Will the payment model ever change?

    No. Flick will not receive a new payment model in the future.However, the price may change in future for sales/after major version updates. Flick is priced with both its current and future value in mind, but new features being added could cause an upward price fluctuation.

  3. Will other platforms ever be supported (Windows, Linux, Mobile)?

    Other platforms are not currently on the roadmap.

  4. What happens if I get a new mac, can my settings be recovered and will I need to pay again?
